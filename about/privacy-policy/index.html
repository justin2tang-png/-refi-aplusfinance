1. Our commitment to protect your privacy

We understand how important it is to protect your personal information. We are committed to treating personal information in a way that protects it and to abiding by the Privacy Principles in the Privacy Act 2020 and other applicable laws.

2. Who are we

References to “we”, “us” and “our” mean A Plus Finance acting through a Financial Adviser.

3. Your authorisation

By providing personal information or engaging us to provide services, you consent to the collection, use, storage and disclosure of personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

4. Changes to our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in law or business needs. By continuing to engage us, you are deemed to have accepted the updated Privacy Policy.

5. What personal information do we collect?

Personal information includes information that identifies, or is capable of identifying, you. This may include your name, date of birth, address, contact details, account details, occupation, financial situation, and goals.

6. Why do we collect your personal information?

We collect personal information to respond to requests, provide services, recommend permitted products, send communications and direct marketing, conduct market research, and for any purpose authorised by you or the Privacy Act. You may opt out of marketing at any time.

7. How do we collect your personal information?

We generally collect information directly from you. We may also collect information from NZ Financial Services Group Limited, credit reporting agencies, banks, employers with your authorisation, product providers, and any other person authorised by you or the Privacy Act.

8. Updating your personal information

We generally rely on you to ensure the information we hold is accurate. If your details change, please contact us as soon as possible.

9. Who do we disclose your personal information to?

We may disclose personal information to NZFSG and related bodies corporate, product providers, prospective lenders, referral partners, contractors, service providers, investors, regulators, government agencies, auditors, and anyone authorised by you or the Privacy Act.

10. Overseas disclosure and cloud storage

We may use cloud storage and IT servers located outside New Zealand. We may also disclose personal information to NZFSG related bodies corporate and overseas third-party suppliers or service providers for the purposes described in the policy.

11. Are you required to provide personal information?

You are not required to provide personal information, but choosing not to may affect our ability to provide services or your ability to obtain finance, insurance, or other products.

12. Access and correction

You may access and request correction of personal information we hold about you by contacting us. We may charge a fee for reasonable retrieval and supply costs.

13. Further information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us. This Privacy Policy was last updated in March 2021.
