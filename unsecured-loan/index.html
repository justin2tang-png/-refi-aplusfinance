When unsecured lending is an option — realistic expectations

何时选择无抵押贷款——现实期望

Unsecured personal loans do not require a registered security interest over property or other assets. This makes them accessible to borrowers who do not own a home or who prefer not to use their property as collateral. However, unsecured lending comes with stricter limits and higher costs.

无抵押个人贷款不需要在房产或其他资产上登记担保权益。这使得没有房产或不愿用房产抵押的借款人也能申请。然而，无抵押贷款的额度和限制更严格，成本也更高。

Lenders consider unsecured loans only for borrowers with a strong credit profile. A stable employment history, a demonstrated capacity to save, and a clean credit file are essential. Borrowers with defaults, late payments, or high existing debt relative to income are unlikely to qualify, or will face significantly higher interest rates.

贷款机构只考虑信用状况良好的借款人。稳定的就业历史、良好的储蓄能力以及干净的信用记录至关重要。有违约、逾期还款或现有债务相对收入较高的借款人不太可能获得批准，或者将面临显著更高的利率。

The maximum loan amount for an unsecured personal loan in New Zealand typically ranges from $5,000 to $50,000. A few lenders may go higher for high-income borrowers with exceptional credit, but amounts above $30,000 require thorough proof of repayment capacity. Loan terms are usually one to five years, with shorter terms for smaller amounts.

在新西兰，无抵押个人贷款的最高额度通常在5,000至50,000纽币之间。少数贷款机构可能会为信用极好的高收入借款人提供更高额度，但超过30,000纽币需要充分的还款能力证明。贷款期限通常为一到五年，金额较小的贷款期限更短。

Interest rates for unsecured loans are higher than for secured lending because the lender carries more risk. Annual rates commonly range from 12% to 25% depending on the lender and the borrower's risk grade. Comparison rates that include establishment and ongoing fees can push the effective cost higher, so borrowers should look at the total cost over the full term rather than the monthly payment alone.

无抵押贷款的利率高于抵押贷款，因为贷款机构承担的风险更大。年利率通常在12%至25%之间，具体取决于贷款机构和借款人的风险等级。包含设立费和持续费用的比较利率可能会推高实际成本，因此借款人应关注整个期限内的总成本，而非仅看每月还款额。

Unsecured loans are best suited for smaller, short-term needs: consolidating credit card debt, covering an unexpected expense such as vehicle repairs, or funding a purchase where the loan is repaid within two to three years. They are not a practical solution for large-scale debt problems or for funding a business or investment property.

无抵押贷款最适合较小、短期的需求：整合信用卡债务、支付意外开支（如车辆维修），或为两到三年内能还清的购买提供资金。它们不是大规模债务问题或为商业或投资房产融资的实用解决方案。

Unsecured vs secured — cost, limits, and approval differences

无抵押与有抵押——成本、额度和审批差异

Choosing between a secured and an unsecured loan depends on what you need the money for, how much you need, and what assets you hold. The two products serve different purposes and come with different trade-offs.

选择有抵押贷款还是无抵押贷款，取决于您需要资金的目的、金额以及您持有的资产。两种产品服务于不同目的，各有不同的利弊权衡。

Secured loans are backed by collateral — typically a property, a vehicle, or term deposits. Because the lender can recover the loan amount by selling the asset if you default, the risk is lower, and this is reflected in the interest rate. Secured personal loans or top-ups on a home loan may carry rates as low as 6-10%, compared to 12-25% for unsecured loans. Loan amounts for secured lending are higher, limited mainly by the available equity in the asset.

有抵押贷款由抵押品担保——通常是房产、车辆或定期存款。因为如果借款人违约，贷款机构可以通过出售资产收回贷款金额，风险较低，这反映在利率上。有抵押个人贷款或住房贷款增额可能低至6-10%，而无抵押贷款则为12-25%。有抵押贷款的金额更高，主要受限于资产中的可用净值。

Approval for a secured loan is often easier if you have sufficient equity, even if your credit history has minor issues. The lender relies on the security rather than solely on your income and repayment history. However, the application process takes longer because the lender needs a valuation and legal documentation to register the security interest.

如果您有足够的资产净值，即使信用历史存在小问题，有抵押贷款也更容易获批。贷款机构依赖抵押品而非仅依赖您的收入和还款历史。然而，申请流程需要更长时间，因为贷款机构需要进行估值并准备法律文件以登记担保权益。

Unsecured loans approve faster. Many lenders offer pre-approval within hours and same-day or next-day funding. No valuation or legal registration is needed. The trade-off is a lower maximum amount, a higher interest rate, and stricter credit criteria. Borrowers with a modest credit score may find that unsecured options are simply not available from mainstream lenders.

无抵押贷款审批更快。许多贷款机构在数小时内即可提供预批，并可在当天或次日放款。无需估值或法律登记。代价是最高额度较低、利率较高、信用标准更严格。信用评分一般的借款人可能会发现主流贷款机构根本不提供无抵押选项。

Fees also differ. Secured loans may involve valuation fees, legal fees for security documentation, and early repayment charges if the loan is paid out within a fixed period. Unsecured loans often have higher establishment fees and may charge ongoing monthly account fees, which can add significantly to the total cost over the term.

费用也各不相同。有抵押贷款可能涉及估值费、担保文件的法律费用，以及在固定期限内提前还款的手续费。无抵押贷款通常设立费更高，并可能收取持续的月度账户管理费，这可能在贷款期限内显著增加总成本。

The right choice depends on your situation. If you own a home and need $30,000 or more, a secured option is likely cheaper overall. If you need $10,000 quickly and do not want to involve your property, an unsecured loan may be the practical answer — provided your credit profile supports it.

正确的选择取决于您的具体情况。如果您拥有房产且需要30,000纽币或以上，有抵押选项总体可能更便宜。如果您急需10,000纽币且不希望动用房产，无抵押贷款可能是实际答案——前提是您的信用状况支持。

Consolidating debt with an unsecured loan — pros and cons

用无抵押贷款整合债务——利弊分析

Debt consolidation means taking out a single loan to pay off multiple existing debts, leaving you with one monthly payment instead of several. An unsecured personal loan can serve this purpose, but it works best in specific circumstances.

债务整合是指借入一笔贷款来偿还多笔现有债务，使您只需每月支付一笔款项而非多笔。无抵押个人贷款可以用于此目的，但它在特定情况下效果最佳。

The main advantage is simplicity. Instead of juggling multiple repayment dates, interest rates, and minimum payments across credit cards, store cards, and personal loans, you have one lender and one regular payment. This reduces the risk of missing a payment accidentally.

主要优势是简单。无需在信用卡、商店卡和个人贷款之间处理多个还款日期、利率和最低还款额，只需面对一个贷款机构和一个定期还款。这降低了意外错过还款的风险。

If your existing debts are on high-interest credit cards with rates of 18-22%, consolidating into an unsecured loan at 13-16% can lower the total interest cost, provided the loan term is not extended too far. A shorter term means higher monthly payments but a lower total cost. A longer term lowers the monthly payment but may increase total interest paid compared to keeping the cards, especially if you continue using them.

如果您现有的债务是利率18-22%的高息信用卡，整合为13-16%的无抵押贷款可以降低总利息成本，前提是贷款期限没有过度延长。较短的期限意味着月供较高但总成本较低。较长的期限降低月供，但与保留信用卡相比可能增加总利息支出，特别是如果您继续使用信用卡。

There are real risks. An unsecured consolidation loan does not address the underlying spending habits that created the debt. Many borrowers who consolidate and keep their credit cards available end up with both a new loan and new card balances within 12 months — a situation that is harder to manage than the original debt. For this reason, some lenders require borrowers to close or reduce credit card limits as a condition of the consolidation loan.

存在实际风险。无抵押整合贷款并不能解决产生债务的根本消费习惯。许多借款人在整合后仍保留信用卡，结果在12个月内同时拥有新贷款和新卡债——这种情况比原始债务更难管理。因此，一些贷款机构要求借款人关闭或降低信用卡额度作为整合贷款的条件。

Interest rates on unsecured consolidation loans are not always lower than the existing debt. If your credit file has missed payments or high utilisation, the offered rate may be closer to 20%, which is similar to a card rate. Running the numbers before applying is essential. A simple comparison of the total repayment amount over the loan term against the total repayment on the current debts will show whether consolidation actually saves money.

无抵押整合贷款的利率并不总是低于现有债务。如果您的信用记录有逾期或高使用率，获批的利率可能接近20%，与信用卡利率相当。在申请前做好数字计算至关重要。将贷款期限内的总还款额与当前债务的总还款额进行简单比较，就能看出整合是否真的省钱。

Lenders also consider your debt-to-income ratio. If the total of existing debts plus the new loan exceeds around 40-50% of gross income, approval is unlikely regardless of the purpose. Borrowers with a debt-to-income ratio above this level may be better served by a secured loan with a lower rate or by speaking with a budget adviser first.

贷款机构还会考虑您的债务收入比。如果现有债务加上新贷款的总和超过总收入的40-50%，无论用途如何，都很难获批。债务收入比超过这一水平的借款人，更适合选择利率较低的有抵押贷款，或先咨询预算顾问。

When structured correctly — matching or reducing the total interest cost, closing the revolving credit facilities, and committing to a realistic repayment term — an unsecured consolidation loan can be a useful tool. It is not a solution for systemic over-indebtedness, and borrowers should seek advice if they are unsure whether consolidation will genuinely improve their position.

如果结构设计得当——匹配或降低总利息成本、关闭循环信用额度、并承诺切实可行的还款期限——无抵押整合贷款可以成为一个有用的工具。但它不是系统性过度负债的解决方案，如果借款人不确定整合是否能真正改善其财务状况，应寻求专业建议。
